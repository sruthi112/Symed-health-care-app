DIARRHOEA

How is it caused?

Diarrhea is caused by increased secretion of fluid into the intestine, reduced absorption of fluid from the intestine or rapid passage of stool through the intestine. Symptoms associated with diarrhea include abdominal pain, especially cramping.

How can I prevent it and cure it?

  • Drink only bottled water, even for tooth brushing.
  • Avoid eating food from street vendors.Avoid ice made with tap water
  • Eat only those fruits or vegetables that are cooked or can be peeled.
