COUGH

How is it caused?

A cough occurs when cells along the air passages get irritated and trigger a chain of events. The result is air in the lungs is forced out under high pressure. A person can choose to cough (a voluntary process), or the body may cough on its own (an involuntary process)

How can I prevent it and cure it?

  • Use air filters and allergy-proof your room,Prevent cockroaches
  • Rest and take decongestants for a cold
  • Incline the head of your bed,It’s easier for irritants to make their way to your throat to trigger coughing when you’re lying down.
    Try propping up some pillows to raise your head.
